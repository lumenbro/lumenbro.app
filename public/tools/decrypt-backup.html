Decrypt LumenBro Backup (.lbk)

This tool runs entirely in your browser. No data leaves your device. Use it to decrypt a previously downloaded encrypted backup created by the LumenBro Mini App.

Tip: For safest handling, disconnect from the internet while decrypting and store your keys offline.

Decrypted Wallet Data

Only download plaintext if you must; keep it offline and secure.
Format: PBKDF2-SHA256 (100k iterations) derives a 256-bit key, used with AES-256-GCM. The .lbk JSON contains salt, iv, and ciphertext.
